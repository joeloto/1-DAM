About Music

Radiohead

Radiohead is a music band of alternative rock and founded in th 80s. Radiohead made his debut with the album "Pablo Honey" in 1993. However, his most famous albums are "Ok Computer" (1997), which contains songs like "Let Down" or "Paranoid Android", and "In Rainbows" (2007), with hits like "Weird Fishes/Arpeggi" or "Jigsaw Falling Into Place". His latest album was "A Moon Shaped Pool" released in 2016. The band members are Thom Yorke (singer), Jonny Greenwood (guitar), Ed O'Brien(guitar), Colin Greenwood (bass) and Phil Selway (drum set).

Post Malone

Austin Richard Post better know as Post Malone, is an american singer, more specifically a rapper. This artist became famous in the second half os the 2010s. He has done a lot of collaborations with other singers, also, Post Malone makes songs in different genres, like rap, cloud rap, rock, melodic rap, trap or pop. One of his most popular albums is "Hollywood's Bleeding" (2019), with songs like "Circles" or "Sunflower", this last song was made for a movie called "Spider-Man:Into The Spider-Verse", released in 2018. This album contains a lot of collaborations with other artists. Other famous records are "Beerbongs & Bentleys" (2018) and "Austin" (2023), this last one with no collaborations, just his voice. This year the american singer released another album called "F-1 Trillion".
